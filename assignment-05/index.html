Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 medium sized ripe avocado
  • Sriracha
  • salt and pepper

    • Directions

    1. start off by toasting both slices of bread
    2. While the bread is tosting, start slicing the avocado.To do this, start slicing the acovado in half vertically. Once the avocado is open, you must remove the pit. To to so, jam the knif into the pit and twist.
    3. Once the avocado is open and depitted, then you can scoop the avocado into a bowl and smash it up
    4. Once the bread is toasted, spread the smashed avocado evenly on both pieces of bread
    5. Top with salt, pepper, and sriracha to taste
