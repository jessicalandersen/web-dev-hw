About Shel Silverstein

Shel Silverstein was a poet and cartoonist mainly known for writing children's poetry. He was born in Chicago in 1930 and wrote many poetry books in his lifetime. Silverstein has received many awards for his work including two Grammy's and nominations for Academy Awards and Golden Globes. I owned two of his poetry books growing up, A Light in the Attic and Where the Sidewalk Ends. Silverstein died in 1999 at the age of 68
