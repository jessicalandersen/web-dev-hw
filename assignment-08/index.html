How to Create Winged Eyeliner

Creating a perfect wing can be tricky. It requires a steady hand and lots of practice. Here's a step by step guide for beginners on how to create the perfect wing.

Everything you'll need to create the wing

  • Liquid eyeliner of choice (pen style liners are the easiest to work with)
  • hand held mirror
  • flat surface to rest elbow on
  • patience

    • Step 1

    start out by creating a line that points up slightly towards the eyebrow

    Step 2

    Starting from about 2/3 of the way on the inital line, draw a line that connects it up onto the eyelid

    Step 3

    Fill in the empty space between the two lines and bring the line on the lid as far in as desired
